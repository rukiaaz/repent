What Is Balance?

Balance is a Discord antinuke and security bot engineered to make server destruction impossible. It monitors every privileged action through the audit log — before any damage can take hold.

Unlike bots that rely on intent detection or message scanning, Balance hooks directly into Discord's audit log — meaning even bots with Administrator cannot bypass it. Mass-deletes, channel nukes, role wipes — all caught and reversed in under a second.

Built for communities that have something worth protecting.
